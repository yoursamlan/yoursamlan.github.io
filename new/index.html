Who am I ?

Hey there!

    My Name is   Amlan.   In Bengali, it refers to someone who always smiles. Believe me or not, I fit that description perfectly :)

    Anyway, let me tell you my story. I originally wanted to be a mathematician, so I went to one of the 162-year-old prestigious colleges in India to become just that. A few things led to another, and I have become a designer who also codes without ever planning to become one of them.

    In addition to designing graphics, UI/UX, etc., and creating beautiful websites, I enjoy taking pictures, playing chess, and automating monotonous tasks with Python. So, feel free to challenge me on the 64 squares if you get bored. And hey, don't forget to share your thoughts after checking out my works, especially my photography :)

Thank you, cheers!!
